About us

IEEE-CBIT is a student-run platform to foster technological i nnovations and nourish excellence in the rising technical community. This renowned student branch of IEEE consists of three societies namely, Power and Energy Society (PES), Computer Society (CS), Signal Processing Society (SPS), and an affinity group, Women In Engineering (WIE). Its also praised for being an awardee of the prestigious ‘Upcoming Student Branch award’ in the Hyderabad Section Student Congress held in December 2017.

The student organization, which works under the supervision of the student executive board formed annually in college, deals with the internal affairs and the operations of the group. This establishment is especially known for its transparency and its inclusive nature regarding decision making and moving forward. There are several departments under this club that are structured cohesively for the smooth operating of all the activities under its banner.

Established in 1993, this student branch offers a wide range of learning and career enhancement opportunities within engineering, science, and research to its members and fellow students. Being one of the most active branches in Hyderabad, IEEE-CBIT strives to fulfill the vision and mission of the IEEE global community for the benefit of humanity. So far it has been successful in helping students shape their careers by doing research and paper publishing on a global scale. Vision, Mission, and exhibition are the three pillars of IEEE-CBIT firmly embedded on its foundation.
